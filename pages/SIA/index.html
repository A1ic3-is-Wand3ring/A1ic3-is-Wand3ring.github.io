Overview



Vision



SIA aims to empower women and girls to stop tackle the poverty cycle and provide a means to a better future, so that they contribute positively to the community and nation.



Mission



Our mission is to engage in impact-driven, sustainable projects that contribute to women development and providing solutions to economic and social issues women face within South Africa.



Objective



To empower women and girl to understand the power and influence they have in shaping the communities and the nation.
To empower women and girls to gain self-worth through self-realisation and thus have confidence and dignity (among others, and not accept and tolerate abuse)
