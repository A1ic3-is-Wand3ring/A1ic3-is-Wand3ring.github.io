Service Offerings

Ethics and Integrity are critical for the success and sustainability of any organisation or company. The application of the values and principles in the organisation is evident in the policies, programs and decisions that are made and implemented. Therefore ethics and integrity have an impact on the morale of employees, productivity and reputation of the organisation.

It is important that there is an alignment between what the organisational purpose is, what the organisation stands for and what the organisation implements and delivers. Based on this, Madutshe apply a three pronged approach, which advocates for constructive dialogue at all levels and leadership. This is a holistic approach in fostering a culture of integrity in the organisation or company, the following are our service offerings:



+ Leadership integrity

It is the Leadership that approves the governance system (which is the first line of defence for any organisation) for the organisation, which includes approving policies and programs to be implemented. How Leadership manages its employees impacts on the employees’ morale. Ethical leadership is not only required by King Code but is also critical for the reputation of the organisation.

+ Employee integrity

Employees are the critical line of defence for an organisation because how they conduct themselves and deliver on their duties determines the quality of services delivered and impacts the reputation of the organisation. It is important that employees make ethical decisions and have a moral courage to do the right thing in spite of the environmental pressures experienced.

+ Organisational culture

Leadership determines the organisational culture through the behavioural standards set (Code of ethics and values) and the tone set through the sanctioning and rewards system (disciplinary, rewards and incentives process). To realise a culture of integrity, leadership must be exemplary and exhibit the behaviour they expected from employees.
