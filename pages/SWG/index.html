Our Delivery Model


SWG provides specialised technical advisory services with regards to Public Private Partnerships (PPP). The PPP model is a strategic partnership between public and private enterprises to develop, build, finance, operate and maintain public facilities such as large hospitals, schools, airports and highways. A consortium shares the risks involved in the project which usually runs for 25-50 years. The return on investment is realised through user fees (tolls) or government payment or a combination of the two.

How we create value

The project is usually funded by a private organisation, this in turn allows for the costs of the project to the public to be spread over a greater period of time. Value is realised in SWG PPP models because of:

  • An established total cost.
    This serves as an insurance against financial surprises.
  • A fixed annual price.
    The customer pays agreed compensation over a predetermined period.
  • Delivery on time with the right quality.
    Compensation is only paid once the facility goes into operation.
  • Frees up resources.
    Having a counterparty with overall responsibility allows the customer to focus on its core operations.
