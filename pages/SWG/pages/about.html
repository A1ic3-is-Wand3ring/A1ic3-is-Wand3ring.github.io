About Us




South West Group participates in infrastructure development projects using PPP model. The group assumes substantial financial, technical and operational risk in the design, financing, building and operations of projects undertaken. The group invests, develops and operates educational infrastructure, health care infrastructure, roads airports and other infrastructure in partnership with the public sector.


South West Group pre-packages projects on behalf of clients, private and public such that the group becomes a single point of management and accountability.

Our Vision and Values


The company envisions to be a market leader in the African continent in developing, providing and managing public infrastructure, recognised for a distinctive approach that delivers outstanding results for clients, partners, communities and the environment.

Integrity



Acting honestly with integrity. South West Group prides itself in being able to do the right thing and behave properly regardless of circumstances. This is value that is built into the company’s DNA and we carry it through even the background processes which are no always visible to monitors.
To achieve this, the company takes a zero-tolerance approach to bribery and corruption and operates with respect and concern for the environment, communities and all other stakeholders.

Safety



Safety is a priority and ensuring and safe and healthy working environment is key to attaining accident free work places. South West Group does not compromise on safety standards and strives to make sure that everyone goes home safely at the end of each working day. South West Group’s Health and Safety performance plan is also one of its competitive advantage

Success



SWG aims to operate a commercially successful business. Success to the company means providing strong returns its shareholders while consistently delivering quality projects to satisfied clients. The company’s “can-do” approach enables it to develop long-term, mutually beneficial relationships and to become a partner of choice.

Teamwork



SWG believes that to achieve its objectives successfully it has to have efficient collaboration of its employees. South West Group will develop its employees through training, reward performance and creating a working environment in which employees are challenged, motivated, satisfied and accountable for their work.

Health care infrastructure



SWG provides refurbishment services of hospital facilities and turnkey hospital construction with strategic partners.
We develop innovative ways to improve the efficiency and sustainability of healthcare systems. Advances in technology also play a major part in improving access to and delivery of healthcare within the community. SWG provides services to extend the life cycle of built facilities and improve sustainability of projects.

Educational infrastructure

SWG engages in the development and construction of tertiary educational facilities and urban living communities. The quality of learning environments is an important contributor to student outcomes as such we have created a smart solutions that are support sustainable urban development. Facilities may be inadequate in many ways, including being over-crowded or dangerous, lacking in adequate sanitary facilities, lacking water for hygiene and lacking modern equipment that supports advanced learning. The implications of these inadequacies are very serious not only for students but for the development of the institution in providing quality education.

Renewable energy

In the wake of global awareness on climate change and sustainable renewable energy development; South West Group’s tailored its approach to meet the demands on evolving climate policies and renewable generation. These solutions help reduce greenhouse gas emissions which are contributing to the deterioration of our environment.
The Company has adopted a unique focus on the Three S Principle of:

  • Ecological Sustainability – Reduction of Carbon Emissions,
  • Economic Sustainability – Creation of Local Jobs; and,
  • Social Sustainability – Bringing production facilities to Townships.

SWG Provides
  • Renewable energy and rural electrification.
  • Cogeneration and decentralised energy systems.
  • Biogas production and energy recovery from wastewater, landfill and MSW.
  • Energy efficiency
  • Biofuels
  • Climate change, Greenhouse Gas (GHG) mitigation/adaptation
  • GHG accounting
